If you are interested in resource disaggregation, memory systems, or any topics related to computer architecture, please reach out to Professor Hyokeun Lee through email. 
If you are not familiar with these topics, I suggest you read a couple of our published papers published in recent 2 or 3 years, so that we can discuss the topics that you are going to work on. 

Applicants need to meet the criteria below: 
- Your motivation and honest personality are the utmost aspects
- Undergraduate research internship in our group
- TEPS / TOEFL: 330 (new TEPS) / 610 (old TEPS) / 85 (TOEFL IBT)
- Mandatory programming language skills: C or C++, Python (or other script languages like perl)
- Recommended language skills:  Verilog or VHDL or SystemVerilog
- Prerequisite major classes: Computer Architecture, Operating System, Digital System Design, Data Structure and Algorithms

**Notes**: the criteria apply to the candidates of Ph.D. or M.S. program, not undergradate internship.
